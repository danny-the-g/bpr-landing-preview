What's inside the community

No app to download. No dashboard to log into. You're added to the community and you train.

  • Vocal projection

    Loud "eeeee" and full-volume work to unstifle your voice, so you carry a room instead of shrinking in it.

  • Emotional range and tonality

    The same line run through apathy, grief, anger, and joy. Rapport tonality, deep self-hypnotic delivery, expressive control on command.

  • Fast talking under pressure

    Non-stop yap while others interrupt and diss you (calibrated 1 to 10 beforehand). You stay sharp and composed while being pushed.

  • Sales practice

    Sell me something, usually a made-up product for fun. Rounds of 60 seconds, then 30, then 20, to force the pitch hard-hitting and to the point.

  • Frame battles

    Two people go head to head, each holding their own frame while the other tries to knock them off it. Builds composure and staying grounded under pressure.

  • Spontaneity and flow

    Free association, merge, yes-and improv, storytelling out of the blue with emotion. You stop scripting and start flowing.

  • Humour and wit

    Super expressive about something boring, super boring about something crazy, playing off other people fast. You get funnier without forcing it.

  • Killing self-consciousness

    Controlled roasting with a hard stop word, and absurd noises that make caring what people think impossible. You build real non-neediness, not a fake front.

  • Self-review loop

    Record across several days, listen back, run it again. Then stop for a week or two, come back, and see exactly where you were forcing and try-harding.

  • More named drills in the rotation

    Magic 8-ball, the 5 game, shotgun, vocal stretch, vocal cadence, range of expression, and vacuum. New drills get added as the community grows.

No. BPR is group coaching exercise calls, multiple students on every call, not one-on-one sessions. That's how we keep it at $47/month instead of hundreds per hour. If you want individual feedback, you can still ask questions live on calls or in the community.

What if I can't make a call?

There are 5+ calls a week, so missing one doesn't set you back the way a monthly single-session program would. Just show up to the next one.

What times are the calls? I'm not in the US.

Calls run at different times across the week specifically so members in different timezones can always find one that works. Wherever you are, there's a call you can make.

The drills are built to meet you where you are. Beginners build the fundamentals; people who already communicate well use the calls to sharpen range, wit, and presence under pressure.
