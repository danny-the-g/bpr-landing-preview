Questions before you join

Is this 1:1 coaching?

No. BPR is group exercise calls, multiple students on every call, not one-on-one sessions. That's how we keep it at $47/month instead of hundreds per hour. If you want individual feedback, you can still ask questions live on calls or in the community.

What if I can't make a call?

There are 5 calls a week, so missing one doesn't set you back the way a monthly single-session program would. Just show up to the next one.

Do I need to download anything?

No. Everything runs through a single invite link. Pay, get your invite, and you're in the community.

What level do I need to be at?

The drills are built to meet you where you are. Beginners build the fundamentals; people who already communicate well use the calls to sharpen range, wit, and presence under pressure.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. It's a monthly membership with no contract. Cancel whenever you want and you won't be billed again.
